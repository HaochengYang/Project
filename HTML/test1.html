Welcome to We Collect You IT

We offer a free collection service across the UK for the removal of your old IT system. We safely reuse this equipment by ensuring any sensitive or personal information that was on these machines is either physilly removed or destroyed before we reuse them. Using us shall not only benefit environment by avoiding the waste and disposal of this equipment but shall also help others, as we donate and proportion of all the equipment we collect.
